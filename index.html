About the Band

The Bowen Military Band is about drive, determination and creativity. It is a manifestation of the school’s vision of shaping self-belief and aspirations. The success of the Bowen Military Band is an exemplification of our school’s foundation of values that bolster students’ holistic education. Their experience with the Band will shape students’ self-belief so that they will be ready to serve their community, their nation and neighbours in this globalised world.

Our Achievements!

  • The band was conferred 16 consecutive gold awards for the past 29 years and being one of only two schools to receive the Gold with Honours award in SYF Central Judging for Display Bands 2010.
  • Furthermore, the band has been invited to perform in public and overseas events, such as the 2013 Taoyuan Band Festival in December.
  • In 2015, the band performed as a guest band at the NCDCC Day parade and were honoured to take part in SG50 Youth Celebrate.
  • In 2017, not only did the band achieve their very first distinction in Concert Band & Percussion Ensemble in SYF Arts Presentation, they were invited to perform at the Formula 1 starting grid presentation.
  • In 2018, the band was invited to perform at the Treasury (Prime Minister's Office) for the year's national day.
  • In 2019, the band had their inaugural concert, Exuberance I, at SOTA Concert Hall with all tickets sold out.
  • The band was also honoured to be invited to perform for our nation during the 2019 national day parade alongside Deyi Military Band.
